Learn About Currencies

Currencies are systems of money in common use, especially for nations. Each currency has a specific code and value that fluctuates based on the global economy. Here are some interesting facts about currencies:

  • USD (United States Dollar): The most widely used reserve currency globally.
  • EUR (Euro): The official currency of 19 of the 27 European Union member states.
  • JPY (Japanese Yen): Known for its stability and widespread use in trading.
  • GBP (British Pound Sterling): The oldest currency still in use.
  • AUD (Australian Dollar): Often called the "Aussie," it is heavily influenced by commodity prices.
